🏡 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI Dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb's global performance across major cities, uncover market trends, evaluate customer satisfaction, and identify growth opportunities through data-driven insights.

📌 Project Overview

This project explores Airbnb listing data from 10 major cities worldwide. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of:

Listing growth over time
Market share by city
Property type distribution
Pricing trends
Host performance
Customer ratings and reviews

The objective is to transform raw Airbnb data into actionable business insights that can help understand market dynamics and customer preferences.

📊 Dashboard Pages

1️⃣ Overview Dashboard

Provides a high-level summary of Airbnb's performance.

Key Metrics:
279.7K+ Listings
182K+ Hosts
10 Cities
144 Property Types
5M+ Reviews
Insights:
Airbnb experienced rapid growth between 2011 and 2015.
2015 marked the peak year for new listings.
Growth slowed after regulatory restrictions in 2016–2017.
Recovery began in 2018 before being disrupted by COVID-19.

2️⃣ Market Share & Ratings Dashboard

Analyzes city-wise market dominance and customer ratings.

Key Insights:
Paris, New York, and Sydney account for nearly 50% of total listings.
These cities contribute approximately 59% of total reviews.
Paris leads in both listings and reviews.
Entire Place listings dominate the market.
Hotel Rooms command the highest average prices.
Average Property Prices:
Property Type	Avg Price
Hotel Room	$800
Entire Place	$673
Shared Room	$580
Private Room	$462

3️⃣ Detailed Ratings Analysis

Breaks down ratings across multiple dimensions:

Accuracy
Cleanliness
Communication
Location
Value
Key Findings:
Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro received the highest overall ratings.
Hong Kong and Istanbul recorded comparatively lower ratings.
Cleanliness and Value consistently scored lower than other metrics.
Communication and Location were the strongest-rated categories.

📈 Key Business Insights

✅ Airbnb reached its highest growth phase in 2015.

✅ Paris remains the strongest market in terms of listings and customer engagement.

✅ Entire Place accommodations are the most preferred property type globally.

✅ Hotel Rooms generate the highest revenue potential due to premium pricing.

✅ Customer satisfaction is highest in Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro.

✅ Cleanliness and Value present opportunities for hosts to improve guest experience.
